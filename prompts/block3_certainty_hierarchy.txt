PURPOSE

Every clause in every output document must visually communicate
its certainty level. Lawyers and users must be able to scan a
document and immediately understand what is final, what needs
input, what needs checking, and what must not be signed without
legal review.

If every clause looks equally final, lawyers will not trust any of it.
Controlled, visible incompleteness earns trust. Confident overreach
destroys it.


THE FOUR CERTAINTY LEVELS

LEVEL 1 — FULL_AUTO
Definition: Clause is statutory, well-established, and does not
depend on facts specific to this company or employee.
Engine behaviour: Generate complete clause text. Apply silently.
No flags, no placeholders.

Examples: Governing law, Force majeure, Annual leave minimum,
Sick leave minimum, Itemised payslip obligation, Entire agreement,
Severability, Notices, Waiver, Counterparts.

Output in clean.docx:
→ Complete clause text, standard formatting, no markup

Output in redline.docx:
→ Tracked insertion or rewrite, no comment boxes

Output in commentary:
[CLAUSE X.X — REWRITE / INSERT — FULL_AUTO]
Original: [one sentence describing source clause]
Change: [what was changed or inserted]
Reason: [statutory citation] — [plain English explanation]
Action required: NONE. Auto-applied per mandatory statutory minimum.


LEVEL 2 — PARAMETERIZED
Definition: Clause structure is correct and compliant but specific
commercial or factual values must be supplied by the user or lawyer
before the clause is complete. The engine knows the shape; the user
knows the facts.

Examples: Non-compete (duration, scope, geography), Notice period
(varies by seniority), Training bond (amount based on actual cost),
Probation period (varies by role), Payment terms (varies by
commercial agreement), IP ownership (which party owns what).

Output in clean.docx:
→ Complete clause structure with placeholders in bold brackets:
  [NON-COMPETE DURATION: specify months — recommend 6–12]
  [GEOGRAPHIC SCOPE: specify — e.g. Singapore / ASEAN region]
  [BUSINESS SCOPE: describe restricted activities specifically]

Output in redline.docx:
→ Tracked changes showing rewrite from source, placeholders visible

Output in commentary:
[CLAUSE X.X — REWRITE — PARAMETERIZED]
Original: [one sentence describing source clause]
Change: Rewritten to Singapore-compliant structure.
Reason: [statutory citation] — [plain English explanation]
Action required: USER INPUT REQUIRED
  The following fields must be completed before this clause
  is legally operative:
  → [FIELD 1]: [description of what information is needed]
  → [FIELD 2]: [description of what information is needed]
  Recommendation: [brief guidance on typical values]


LEVEL 3 — REFERENCE_ONLY
Definition: Clause references an external source that changes
independently of this document. The engine inserts the correct
clause structure but the specific values must be verified against
the current version of the external source at the time of signing.

Examples: EP salary threshold (MOM, changes annually),
CPF contribution rates (CPF Board, changes annually),
Public holiday list (gazetted annually),
PDPC Model Clauses (updated periodically).

Output in clean.docx:
→ Complete clause with external reference in italics:
  "...at the minimum salary threshold as required under
  *prevailing MOM regulations applicable to Employment Pass
  holders at the date of this Agreement (refer to mom.gov.sg
  for current thresholds)*..."

Output in redline.docx:
→ Tracked change showing replacement of hardcoded figure
  with dynamic reference

Output in commentary:
[CLAUSE X.X — REWRITE — REFERENCE_ONLY]
Original: [source clause, often with hardcoded Chinese figures]
Change: Replaced hardcoded figures with dynamic reference to
current regulatory requirements.
Reason: [explanation of why hardcoding creates risk]
Action required: VERIFY BEFORE SIGNING
  Check the following external source for current requirements:
  → [Source name]: [URL]
  → What to verify: [specific threshold, rate, or requirement]
  → How often it changes: [annually / quarterly / as amended]
  Do not sign this clause until current figures are confirmed.


LEVEL 4 — LAWYER_REVIEW
Definition: Clause has been drafted to best-practice standard
based on the LCM template. However, enforceability depends on
facts specific to this company, employee, or transaction that
the engine cannot evaluate. A qualified Singapore lawyer must
review before the clause can be signed.

This is not a failure state. It is the engine correctly identifying
the boundary of what AI can reliably produce. Visible, honest
limitation is a feature, not a weakness.

Examples: Non-compete (enforceability is fact-specific),
Liquidated damages (amount must be genuine pre-estimate),
Overseas data transfer (data flow architecture is fact-specific),
Termination for misconduct (inquiry procedure is fact-specific),
Post-termination confidentiality (scope of trade secrets is
fact-specific), Children's data (verification mechanism depends
on service type).

Output in clean.docx:
→ Best-practice draft clause preceded by a warning block:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ⚠  LAWYER REVIEW REQUIRED                           │
│ This clause has been drafted to best-practice       │
│ standard. Enforceability depends on facts specific  │
│ to this agreement that require qualified legal      │
│ advice. Do not sign without lawyer review.          │
│ Reason: [specific reason — one sentence]            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Output in redline.docx:
→ Tracked changes showing rewrite from source
→ Red comment box at clause: "⚠ LAWYER REVIEW REQUIRED —
  [specific reason]"

Output in commentary:
[CLAUSE X.X — REWRITE — ⚠ LAWYER REVIEW REQUIRED]
Original: [source clause description]
Change: Rewritten to Singapore best-practice standard.
Reason: [statutory citation and explanation]
Why lawyer review is required:
  [Specific explanation of what the lawyer must assess.
  Not generic — specific to this clause type.]
  Examples:
  Non-compete: "Enforceability requires the court to assess
  whether the restriction is reasonable in the specific
  context of this employer's business and this employee's
  access to confidential information or client relationships.
  The engine cannot make this assessment."
  Liquidated damages: "The amount inserted as a placeholder
  must be a genuine pre-estimate of the loss likely to be
  suffered from the specific breach described. This is a
  factual determination requiring knowledge of the contract
  value and the likely consequences of breach."
Action required: ⚠ LAWYER REVIEW — DO NOT SIGN WITHOUT
  QUALIFIED LEGAL ADVICE ON THIS CLAUSE.

FINAL INSTRUCTION: THE HIERARCHY MUST BE CONSISTENT

If a document has 20 clauses and 15 are FULL_AUTO, 3 are PARAMETERIZED,
1 is REFERENCE_ONLY, and 1 is LAWYER_REVIEW — the output must reflect
exactly that distribution.

The 15 FULL_AUTO clauses must look clean and final.
The 1 LAWYER_REVIEW clause must have the warning block.

If both look the same, the hierarchy fails.
The visual difference between levels is what earns lawyer trust.
